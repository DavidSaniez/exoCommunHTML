Présentation

Sungha Jung est un guitariste sud-coréen né le 2 septembre 1996 à Cheongju en Corée du Sud.

Il s'est fait connaître à l'aide du site web YouTube où ses vidéos ont été vues plus de 1 000 000 000 de fois. Il reprend des classiques de chanteurs et de groupes internationaux, Michael Jackson, Céline Dion, Sting, Green Day, U2..., des classiques de jeux vidéo, Super Mario, The Legend of Zelda..., de cinéma, Pirates des Caraïbes... et crée même ses propres compositions. Ses albums ont été notamment coproduits par de grands guitaristes fingerstyle comme Ulli Bögershausen.

How do i know him?

When I started learning guitar , i was looking for someone talentful AND who plays 'Fingerstyle' guitar. FingerPicking or fingerstyle , is a technique for playing using fingertips and/or finger nails rather than a plectrum. It's used for classical guitar and some other styles. The Main objective for this technique is to play the bass part of the song and at the same time the melody. This makes fingerstyle an impressive technique and a very fun way to learn guitar.

Why you should know him?

From my point of view , he is already a great guitarist but not only that. At age around 14 , he was playing with famous guitarists like Andy Mckee and Tommy Emmanuel. Sungha Jung plays since he's 6 y-old and seeing him play along the most skilled guys on the guitar just gives me the chill everytime. So why don't you look up some songs he played ?
